Two-controller browser adventure

CouchQuest

Player 1 is the hero on the battlefield. Player 2 is the dungeon director, placing enemies, obstacles, and timed challenges with cooldowns.

Press A to start, X to swap controller roles, and press any button on a controller to wake it up.

Hero Controls

Left Stick move

Right Stick aim

A dash

X sword sweep

B pulse burst

Director Controls

Left Stick move the cursor

A spawn a goblin

X place a rock

B drop a hazard

Y start a challenge wave
